Where can I get help using R outside of class time?

Online

  • Chico R User Group Google email list and discussion board.
    • Contribute your own solutions and advice as you can.
  • Stack Overflow using the R tag https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/r
    • Both of these are most helpful when you prepare a minimal working example of your problem for the other users.
  • UCLA IDRE https://stats.idre.ucla.edu/r/
  • Cookbook for R http://www.cookbook-r.com/
  • R Examples repository http://dwoll.de/rexrepos/index.html

In Person:

  • Math Learning Lab
    • MLIB 4th floor
    • Mariby Cruz, Mitch Borgert, Jerry Tucay
